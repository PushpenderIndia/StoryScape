Problem Statement

Nowadays students face problems due to low attention span, which is less than a goldfish (9 sec & humans have 8 sec).
Also, it is very hard to spread awareness about topics which are considered “taboo” in our society such as periods, superstitions, sex education, etc.
As per studies conducted in the US by NCBI, suggested that approx. 65% of the population are visual learners.
So learning from textual content leads to :

Difficulty in Conceptualization

Students find it hard to understand and conceptualize complex topics.

Reduced Retention

Information retention is low due to lack of engagement.

Limited Engagement

Students are less engaged with traditional teaching methods.

Difficulty in Problem-Solving

Students struggle with applying knowledge to solve problems.

Limited Creativity and Expression

There's little room for creative thinking and self-expression.

Increased Cognitive Load

Students experience high cognitive load when learning.

Our Solution

Our idea is to build Gen AI powered platform, which will convert boring textual content or taboo topics to visually appealing comics/manga.

User can specify plot & characters of the storyline or just enter a topic and it will generate a comic book as per their comic style i.e. Marvel, DC, Disney Princess, Anime etc.

Our platform will utilize image-to-image transformations using Stable Diffusion or Gen AI models.

Features

Ability to set custom characters and story plot

Users can personalize their comics by setting their own characters and storyline, adding uniqueness to their creations.

Generate Shareable comic link or share comic pdf

After creating their comic, users can generate a shareable link or download the comic as a PDF file to easily share it with others.

Enhances user experience with realistic animations

The platform provides realistic animations such as page turning and book opening/closing, creating an immersive digital reading environment for users.

Ability to create vernacular comics

Users can create comics in their preferred language, including Hindi, English, Tamil, etc., catering to diverse audiences.
